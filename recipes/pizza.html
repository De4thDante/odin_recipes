Pizzas

Discription

A delicious pizza with extra cheese, tomato base along with a good amount of different meats such as bacon,salami,steak

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Assorted meats of choice
  • Mozzarela
  • Tomato paste
  • Basil
  • Origanum
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps to make

  1. Bloom yeast in lukewarm water
  2. Mix salt, flour and sugar(only if desired, will help with yeast fermentation)
  3. Cook tomato paste with water , then add basil,salt and pepper. Simmer until thickened to sauce
  4. Cook meats if desired (THe meat will be cooked in the oven either way)
  5. Add yeast water to the flour mixture and combine and knead until it's not sticky to the hands, then let rest and rise for 1 hour
  6. Roll dough out into circle (or other shapes if you want) and pre bake it in the oven
  7. Spread tomato sauce on semi-cooked base
  8. Sprinkle on cheese
  9. Add meats and Origanum
  10. Bake in the oven untill cheese is golden brown or crust begins to darken

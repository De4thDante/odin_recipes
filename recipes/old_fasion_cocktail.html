Old Fashioned Cocktail

Discription

A Delightful and easy recipe for a cocktail that spans through the ages ( well as far back presumably ,as a few hundred years ago)

Ingredients

  • Bourbon whiskey
  • Simple Syrup
  • Orange peels
  • Maraschino cherry
  • Bitters
  • water
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Mix water, bitters and simple syrup and stir to combine
  3. Add Ice cubes
  4. Pour in bourbon
  5. muddle the cherry and stir well
  6. Garnish wth orange slice
